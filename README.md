## Image & Design

> AI tools for image generation, upscaling, background removal, logo creation, and illustration.

### Image Generation
- [Stable Diffusion](https://stability.ai) - Open-source text to image AI, free and runs locally. No account required.
- [Adobe Firefly](https://firefly.adobe.com) - AI image generator with commercial-safe training data. Free tier available.
- [Ideogram](https://ideogram.ai) - AI image generator with accurate text rendering. Free plan available.
- [Leonardo.ai](https://leonardo.ai) - AI art generator with fine-tuned models and free daily credits.
- [Playground AI](https://playgroundai.com) - Free AI image generator with 500 images/day on free plan.
- [Craiyon](https://craiyon.com) - Completely free AI image generator, no account required.

### Image Upscaling & Enhancement
- [Upscayl](https://upscayl.org) - Free open-source AI upscaler, runs locally. No watermark, no upload limits. Supports 4x/8x via Real-ESRGAN.
- [Upscale.media](https://upscale.media) - Online AI image upscaler, free tier, no signup required for basic use.
- [Let's Enhance](https://letsenhance.io) - AI photo enhancer and upscaler. Improves quality and resolution in one click.
- [Topaz Gigapixel AI](https://topazlabs.com/gigapixel-ai) - Professional AI image upscaler supporting up to 6x enlargement.
- [VanceAI](https://vanceai.com) - AI image upscaler with batch processing support. Free credits available.
- [Pixelbin](https://pixelbin.io) - AI image enhancement and transformation platform. Free plan available.

### Background Removal
- [Remove.bg](https://remove.bg) - One-click AI background remover. Free for low-resolution output.
- [Clipdrop](https://clipdrop.co) - AI background remover and image editing suite by Stability AI. Free tier available.
- [PhotoRoom](https://photoroom.com) - AI background remover optimized for product photos. Free plan available.
- [Erase.bg](https://erase.bg) - Free AI background remover, no signup required. Supports bulk removal.

### Logo & Branding
- [Looka](https://looka.com) - AI logo generator with full brand kit. Free preview available.
- [Hatchful](https://hatchful.shopify.com) - Free AI logo generator by Shopify. No account required.
- [Brandmark](https://brandmark.io) - AI logo and brand identity generator.
- [Namecheap Logo Maker](https://namecheap.com/logo-maker) - Completely free AI logo generator, no signup required.

### Illustration & Art
- [Midjourney](https://midjourney.com) - Industry-leading AI art generator for photorealistic and artistic images.
- [Krea.ai](https://krea.ai) - Real-time AI illustration generator with style controls. Free plan available.
- [Recraft](https://recraft.ai) - AI illustration and vector art generator. Free plan with generous credits.
- [Canva AI](https://canva.com) - AI image generator integrated into Canva's design platform. Free plan available.

## Writing & Content

> AI tools for copywriting, blogging, essays, email writing, and content generation.

### AI Writing Assistants
- [ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com) - General-purpose AI writing assistant by OpenAI. Free plan available.
- [Claude](https://claude.ai) - AI writing assistant by Anthropic. Strong at long-form and nuanced content. Free plan available.
- [Gemini](https://gemini.google.com) - AI writing assistant by Google, integrated with Google Docs. Free to use.
- [Writesonic](https://writesonic.com) - AI content generator for blogs, ads, and social media. Free plan available.

### Essay & Long-form Writing
- [Jenni AI](https://jenni.ai) - AI essay writer with citation support and plagiarism checker. Free plan available.
- [Rytr](https://rytr.me) - AI writing tool for essays and blogs. Free plan with 10K characters/month.
- [Hyperwrite](https://hyperwrite.ai) - AI writing assistant for essays and long-form documents. Free plan available.
- [Sudowrite](https://sudowrite.com) - AI creative writing and fiction assistant. Free trial available.

### Copywriting
- [Copy.ai](https://copy.ai) - AI copywriting tool for ads and emails. Free plan with unlimited words.
- [Jasper](https://jasper.ai) - AI copywriting platform for marketing teams. Free trial available.
- [Anyword](https://anyword.com) - AI copywriting tool with performance prediction scoring. Free trial available.
- [Simplified](https://simplified.com) - AI copywriting and design tool combined. Free plan available.

### Blog & SEO Content
- [Koala](https://koala.sh) - AI blog post generator optimized for SEO. Free plan available.
- [Frase](https://frase.io) - AI content generator and SEO research tool combined. Free trial available.
- [Seowriting.ai](https://seowriting.ai) - AI SEO content writer with auto internal linking. Free plan available.
- [Scalenut](https://scalenut.com) - AI blog writer with keyword clustering and content planning. Free trial available.

### Paraphrasing
- [Quillbot](https://quillbot.com) - AI paraphrasing tool with 8 writing modes. Free plan with 125 words/paraphrase.
- [Wordtune](https://wordtune.com) - AI rewriting and paraphrasing tool. Free plan with 10 rewrites/day.
- [Paraphraser.io](https://paraphraser.io) - Free AI paraphrasing tool, no account required. Unlimited on free plan.
- [Scribbr Paraphraser](https://scribbr.com/paraphrasing-tool) - Free AI paraphrasing tool for academic writing.

### Email Writing
- [Lavender](https://lavender.ai) - AI email writing assistant for sales outreach. Free plan available.
- [Flowrite](https://flowrite.com) - AI email writer that turns bullet points into full emails. Free trial available.
- [Smartwriter](https://smartwriter.ai) - AI personalized email writer for cold outreach. Free trial available.
- [Ellie](https://tryellie.com) - AI email reply assistant that learns your writing style. Free plan available.

### Product Descriptions
- [Describely](https://describely.ai) - AI product description generator for e-commerce. Free plan available.
- [Hypotenuse AI](https://hypotenuse.ai) - AI product description and content generator. Free trial available.
- [CopyMonkey](https://copymonkey.ai) - AI Amazon listing and product description generator. Free trial available.

## Audio & Music

> AI tools for music generation, voice cloning, transcription, and audio editing.

### Music Generation
- [Suno](https://suno.ai) - AI song generator creating full vocal tracks from text prompts. Free tier available.
- [Udio](https://udio.com) - AI music generator supporting multiple genres, free with no account required.
- [Mubert](https://mubert.com) - Royalty-free AI background music generator. Free plan available.
- [Soundraw](https://soundraw.io) - AI-generated music customizable by mood, genre, and length.
- [Boomy](https://boomy.com) - Create and publish AI-generated songs in seconds. Free tier available.

### Text to Speech
- [ElevenLabs](https://elevenlabs.io) - Ultra-realistic AI voice generator. Free tier with 10K characters/month.
- [Murf](https://murf.ai) - AI text to speech with 120+ natural voices. Free plan available.
- [PlayHT](https://play.ht) - AI voice generator supporting 900+ voices across 142 languages.
- [Kokoro TTS](https://huggingface.co/hexgrad/Kokoro-82M) - Open-source AI text to speech model, free and runs locally.

### Voice Cloning
- [Resemble AI](https://resemble.ai) - AI voice cloning with real-time synthesis. Free trial available.
- [Kits.ai](https://kits.ai) - AI voice cloning for music and content creators. Free tier available.
- [RVC](https://github.com/RVC-Project/Retrieval-based-Voice-Conversion-WebUI) - Open-source real-time voice cloning, runs locally for free.

### Transcription
- [Whisper](https://github.com/openai/whisper) - Open-source AI speech recognition by OpenAI. Free, runs locally.
- [Otter.ai](https://otter.ai) - AI meeting and podcast transcription. Free plan available.
- [Descript](https://descript.com) - AI podcast transcription and audio editor combined.
- [Riverside](https://riverside.fm) - AI podcast recording and transcription platform. Free tier available.

### Audio Enhancement
- [Adobe Podcast](https://podcast.adobe.com) - AI noise cancellation and voice enhancement. Free to use.
- [Krisp](https://krisp.ai) - AI background noise cancellation for calls and recordings. Free plan available.
- [Lalal.ai](https://lalal.ai) - AI audio separator for vocals, instruments, and stems. Free tier available.
- [Demucs](https://github.com/facebookresearch/demucs) - Open-source AI audio source separator by Meta. Free, runs locally.
